Safety, Security, and Privacy of Open-Source Ecosystems (Safe-OSE)

Closed Grant
National Science Foundation (NSF)

$1,500,000 over 24 months; Year 1 not to exceed $500,000

Deadline: Preliminary proposal: 2026-01-13 · Full proposal: 2026-04-28

Supports open-source ecosystems addressing significant vulnerabilities — both technical (code vulnerabilities, side-channels) and socio-technical (supply chain, insider threats, social engineering). Ecosystems do not need prior NSF funding to apply.

Geography
USA
Focus area
Open-source ecosystem safety, security, and privacy
Website

Google.org Impact Challenge: AI for Science

Closed Grant
Google.org

$500,000 – $3,000,000

Deadline: Full application: 2026-04-17

A $30M global open-call that funds researchers and organizations to accelerate scientific breakthroughs with AI. Selected participants may also join a six-month Google.org Accelerator with technical support from Google specialists and Google Cloud credits.

Geography
Global
Focus area
AI for scientific research
Website
