What is CURIOSS?

CURIOSS is a community for those working in University and Research Institution CURIOSSs. Our goals are to facilitate the networking and collaboration between CURIOSS representatives from universities and research institutions worldwide. CURIOSS was set up in 2023 with the support of a grant from the Alfred P. Sloan Foundation.

If you are part of any of the following categories you are welcome to join our Community. We are a friendly bunch.
